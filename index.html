What we do

Four practices.
One relentless focus.

From the first whiteboard session to systems processing millions of requests in production — we're with you across every phase. No handoffs to junior teams. No mystery black boxes. Just outcomes.

01 / Advisory

AI Strategy

We don't sell you a roadmap and disappear. We audit your workflows, challenge your assumptions, and identify the two or three places where AI will create an unfair advantage — then we build the plan to get there in weeks, not quarters. Opinionated, specific, and built for execution from day one.

02 / Build

Implementation

From working prototype to production-grade system. We build custom AI solutions — agents, RAG pipelines, fine-tuned models, automation workflows — integrated into the tools your team already uses. Secure by design. Observable. Yours to own completely, with zero vendor lock-in.

How we work

Built for
the long game.

We don't parachute in, build something, and hand you the keys. We work alongside you — through the build, through the rollout, and through every model upgrade and capability shift that comes after.

01 — DISCOVER

Audit your reality.

We start by understanding how your business actually operates — not the org chart version, the real one. We map your stack, workflows, data, and constraints. We identify where AI creates genuine leverage and where it's hype. Everything that follows is grounded in this diagnostic. No assumptions, no templates.

02 — BUILD

Ship working systems.

We design and build in tight sprints, showing you working software at every checkpoint — not mockups, not wireframes. You pick the models, review the guardrails, and control the deployment timeline. The first system ships fast. Then we iterate, measure, and improve based on what the data actually says.

03 — EVOLVE

Stay as long as you need us.

We don't disappear after launch. We stay — monitoring performance, retraining on your data, upgrading to better models as they release, and expanding what the system can do as your business grows. The AI landscape moves fast. We make sure you're always running the best version, not last year's.
